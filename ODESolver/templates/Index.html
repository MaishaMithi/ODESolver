About ODEsolver

Assalamu Alaikum. We have created a web-based project, that solves ODE (Ordinary Differential Equations), by three methods
They are,
1. Eulers' Method
2. Heuns' Method, and
3. 4th Order Runge-Kutta Method.
We also tried to integrate them on real-life engineering problems.
Which are,
1. Material Science & Engineering (MSE)
2. Mechanical Engineering (ME)
3. Chemical Engineering (ChE)
4. Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
